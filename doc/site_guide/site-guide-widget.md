The **site guide** is the question-mark button in the lower-right corner of the page. Click it to open a chat panel.

Type a question about how to use Canvas or this site's features, then press **Send** or Enter. The guide answers from built-in help articles for common tasks.

The guide knows which page you are on and can give more relevant answers on the Dashboard or inside a course. It does not access other users' data or change your account settings.

If the guide does not know an answer, it will say so instead of guessing.
