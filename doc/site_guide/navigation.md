Use the **global navigation** on the left to move between major areas: Dashboard, Courses, Calendar, Inbox, and Help.

Open **Account** (your avatar or profile menu) for personal settings, notification preferences, and profile details.

Inside a course, the **course navigation** links to Home, Assignments, Grades, Modules, and other tools enabled for that course.

The **Help** link in global navigation opens Canvas support options for your institution. The **site guide** button in the lower-right corner answers questions about using this site and its features.
