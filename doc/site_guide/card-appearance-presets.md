In **Dashboard Card View**, you can choose how course cards look using **appearance presets** (for example classic, bubble, star, or cat).

Open the **Dashboard options** menu while you are in Card View and select a preset. Cards update right away so you can preview the style. Your choice is saved to your account and applies the next time you visit the Dashboard.

If you have not chosen a preset yet, Canvas uses the **classic** style. If a saved preset is no longer supported, Canvas safely falls back to classic.

Changing the appearance preset does not change course order, favorites, or the information shown on each card.
