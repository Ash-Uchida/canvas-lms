The Dashboard is your home page in Canvas. It shows courses you have favorited and gives quick access to recent activity.

To switch how courses are displayed, use the dashboard view options (for example Card View, Planner, or Activity). Card View shows course tiles you can open with one click.

If you do not see a course, open **Courses** in the global navigation and favorite the course from the list. Favorited courses appear on the Dashboard.
